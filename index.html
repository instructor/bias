Bias Identification and Mitigation Framework / Additional Material

last update: Nov 22, 2021 by (Eduard Klein)
Starting in 2019, we developed an awareness-raising framework for bias identification and mitigation, consisting of a meta model and a set of checklists. The framework has been validated in the context of industrial AI projects. This webpage offers additional material for the below mentioned publications.
